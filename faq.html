Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver across Lusaka. Same-day delivery available for orders confirmed before 2pm.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Airtel Money, MTN Money, Cash on delivery, and Bank transfer.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes, all products come with a 30-day warranty against manufacturer defects.

How long does repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. We'll contact you when ready.

Are your products genuine?

Yes, we only sell 100% genuine products from trusted suppliers.

Can I return a product?

Yes, you can return within 7 days if the product is unused and in original packaging.

Do you have a physical shop?

Yes, contact us for our location in Lusaka. We prefer to deliver or meet customers.
